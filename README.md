# Analytical-Solutions-to-Biological-Systems
Included in this repository is my paper about continuous dynamical system models of biological processes and their analytical solutions, as well as the accompanying source code for all figures. In brief, I explore uncoupled and coupled gene cascades as well as PDE models of reaction diffusion and population dynamics.

Below, you can find the abstract for this work:

## Abstract
Many biological processes are abstracted to continuous dynamical systems. In this paper, I solve several foundational ordinary and partial differential equation models of biological systems. In uncoupled biomolecular systems or gene cascades, I often examine molecular activity as a limit of high cooperativity—--which is amenable to solutions via the Laplace transform. Linear systems and linear approximations of cooperative regulation networks of coupled genes are analyzed. Finally, I present general solutions to PDEs governing a reaction-diffusion system and a Fokker-Planck equation.
